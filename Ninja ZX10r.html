Ninja ZX10r

A Little Introduction

The Kawasaki Ninja ZX-10R is a motorcycle in the Ninja sport bike series from the Japanese manufacturer Kawasaki,the successor to the Ninja ZX-9R. It combines an ultra-narrow chassis, low weight, and radial brakes, also Kawasaki has tweaked the ergonomics on the new ZX-10s to result in a more committed riding position.
