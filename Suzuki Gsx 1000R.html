GSX 1000R

A Little Introduction

The Suzuki GSX-R1000R is the first superbike to get Variable Valve Timing (VVT), indigenously designed by Suzuki. VVT results in better mid-range performance, along with extra grunt in the top-end of the power bandA decade and a half since the first GSX-R1000 (K1) changed the open sportsbike class forever. Now, the 6th-generation of Suzuki GSX-R1000 is redefining the definition of “Superbike” claims the company. It embodies the life work and professional ambition of Suzuki engineers who are extremely passionate about the GSX-R brand and its place in motorcycle history.
