RC 200

A Little Introduction

The Duke 200 is manufactured at Bajaj Autos Chakan Plant in India.In Colombia, it is being assembled by the company Auteco S.A .In Argentina, is being assembled by the company Simpa S.A. since March 2014. In the Philippines, it is being assembled by the company KTM Asia Motorcycle Manufacturing, Inc. (KAMMI) in Sta. Rosa, LagunaThe bike has an underbelly exhaust and a three-chamber silencer positioned close to its centre of gravity.
