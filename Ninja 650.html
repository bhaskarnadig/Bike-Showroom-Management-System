Ninja 650

A Little Introduction

The Kawasaki Ninja 650R, also called ER-6f or EХ-6, is a motorcycle in the Ninja series from the Japanese manufacturer Kawasaki.They have modern styling and features, with low-seating ergonomics and a low center of gravity. The Ninja 650 is, essentially, the fully-faired version of the Z650. On the design front, the bike is unmistakably a Ninja, replete with twin headlamps at the front, a sporty front cowl and a muscular fuel tank with Kawasaki branding.
