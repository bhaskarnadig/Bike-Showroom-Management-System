Thunderbird LT1700

A Little Introduction

Thunderbird LT is an elite cruiser,it rivals none other than Harley Davidson Fat Bob and Indian Scout in its segment.In Thunderbird LT - LT stands for 'Light Touring'. the design was by Tim Prentice in California in 2009 and is still being uplifted to newer heights.
