DUCATI 1098S

A Little Introduction

The Ducati 1098 is a sport bike made by Ducati from 2007 to 2009, in three versions.The 1098 shares more design elements with the older 998 than with its predecessor the 999, such as horizontally placed headlights and a non-integrated exhaust system. Another carryover from its 916/998 heritage is the single-sided swingarm. The 1098 was designed by Ducati designer, Giandrea Fabbro.
