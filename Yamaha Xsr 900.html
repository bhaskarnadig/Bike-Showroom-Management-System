XSR 900

A Little Introduction

Yamaha XSR900 is a neo-retro standard Universal Japanese Motorcycle (UJM), which utilizes transverse inline-triple engineYamaha brought the XSR900 to market in 2016, releasing it in two variants worldwide.The first, a variant utilizes a yellow and black paint scheme with gold inverted fork. This paint scheme is a 60th Anniversary Edition first utilized on other Yamaha models in 2015, such as the YZF-R1, to commemorate Yamaha's 60 years of racing heritage which began in 1955 and strongly resembles the classic "speed block" design popularized by Kenny Roberts in the 1970s, while the second variant utilizes a more muted aluminum tank over black frame and black inverted fork.
