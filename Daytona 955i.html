Daytona 955i

A Little Introduction

The Triumph Daytona 955i was introduced in an attempt by Triumph to tap into the sports bike market. Acomplete restyling of the bodywork was done by designer Gareth Davies, the Daytona helped to establish the newly managed manufacturer and its distinctive and unique three-cylinder motorcycles.
