390 Adventure

A Little Introduction

The KTM 390 Adventure was first unveiled at the EICMA Motorcycle show in 2019. The 390 Adventure is, essentially, the adventurous version of the 390 Duke. So, in line with its adventurous intentions, there are a number of changes that set it apart from its sibling. Most importantly, it gets a revamped design. It also gets newer alloy wheels, a large windscreen, and knuckle guards. The fuel tank gets sharper extensions, giving the motorcycle an aggressive stance. Also new to the 390 Adventure are a longer rider seat and a brushed aluminium exhaust.
